### 📊 课程资产大盘

```dataview

  segments as "视频段数", 
  duration as "总时长", 
  srt_lines as "转录行数", 
  frames as "关键帧数",
  created as "生成时间"
WHERE segments != null AND file.name != "INDEX" AND file.name != "000_我的视频知识大盘"
```
